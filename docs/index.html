捐赠 - Donation

开源项目捐赠不是贫困慈善捐赠，也不是工作报酬，而是对开源项目的帮助。这些项目是开源免费的，然而，我独自付出了个人的时间和精力，生活与生存也面临着被社会碾碎的压力，如果能提供资金帮助我的话，我将会有更多的时间和精力继续开源代码的开发和维护。

如果你有特定的开发需求而又找不到特定的解决方案的话，也可以找我定制开发满足你的需求，并支付一定的报酬，报酬的多少过得去就行了。

在此我顺便声明，报酬不是捐赠， 他们并不相同。

Open-Source Project Donation is not poor charity donation, and it is not job salary, it is the help to the open-source project. These projects are open-source and free; however, I contribute my personal time and mental energy lonely, and my life and survival are under the threaten of crashed by the social presure. If you can donate some money to help me, I can make more time and mental energy to develop and maintain the open-source code.

If you have some special code need, but you can not get specific solution, you can also contact me to work for your special need, and pay some wage, any reasonable wage is OK.

And I declare by the way, wage is not donation, they are not the same.

更多的捐赠方式在构建中，也不是所有的捐赠方式都被列出，如果你有其它的未列出的捐赠方式可以私下联系我。

More donation methods are on the building, not every methods are listed. If you have other method unlisted, please contact me privately.

隐私保护：尽量不要追踪我的个人信息，如果你碰巧了解到我的个人信息，也不要随意保存和发给他人吧，大家相互尊重一下吧，这主要是为了防止垃圾骚扰。

Privacy Protection: You'd better not trace my personal information. If you have learned my personal information accidently, please do not store or send to others. We'd better respect each other. Which is mainly for preventing spam harassment.
